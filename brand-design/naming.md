# Game Name: Formateur

## Selected Name
**Formateur**

## Rationale
In Belgian politics, the **Formateur** is the person appointed by the King to form the government. This role is central to the political process and perfectly encapsulates the core gameplay loop of coalition building and government management.

### Key Attributes
*   **Authentic:** Directly references a specific, high-stakes role in Belgian politics.
*   **Strategic:** Implies construction, negotiation, and leadership.
*   **Unique:** Stands out from generic "Tycoon" or "Simulator" titles.

## Logo Concept
The logo (see `logo.png`) features a stylized representation of the parliament hemicycle/fountain pen, incorporating the Belgian tricolor (Black, Yellow, Red) in a sleek, modern, and professional aesthetic.
